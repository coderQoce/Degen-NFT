Topic 14
Glossary

Below are essential crypto terms used in this Degen handbook explained.

Memecoin

A memecoin is a cryptocurrency that becomes popular mainly through internet culture, memes, and community support.

Airdrop

An airdrop is when a crypto project gives free tokens to users to reward early supporters or grow its community.

Blockchain

A blockchain is a digital system that records transactions securely across many computers instead of one central authority.

Base

Base is a blockchain network built to make crypto transactions faster and cheaper than Ethereum.

Layer-2

A Layer-2 network is a blockchain built on top of another blockchain to increase speed and reduce transaction costs.

Token

A token is a digital asset that exists on a blockchain and can be sent, received, or used inside an ecosystem.

Tipping

Tipping means sending small amounts of cryptocurrency to reward someone for their content or contribution.

Creator Economy

The creator economy is a system where people earn money by creating content online, such as posts, videos, or memes.

Liquidity

Liquidity describes how easily a token can be bought or sold in the market without large price changes.

Exchange

A crypto exchange is a platform where users buy, sell, or trade cryptocurrencies.

Smart Contract

A smart contract is a program on a blockchain that automatically executes actions when certain conditions are met.

SocialFi

SocialFi is the combination of social media and decentralized finance, where users earn tokens for online participation.

dApp (Decentralized Application)

A dApp is an application that runs on a blockchain instead of a centralized server.

DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization)

A DAO is a community that makes decisions together using blockchain technology. Instead of one leader controlling everything, members vote on decisions.

NFT (Non-Fungible Token)

An NFT is a unique digital asset stored on a blockchain. Unlike normal tokens, NFTs are not interchangeable.

Explorer

A blockchain explorer is a website that lets users see blockchain transactions.

Bridge

A bridge is a tool that allows tokens to move between different blockchains.

Community Token

A community token is a cryptocurrency created mainly for a community.

Micro transactions

Micro-transactions are very small payments, often worth only a few cents or dollars.

Liquidity Pool

A liquidity pool is a collection of tokens locked in a smart contract that helps enable trading on decentralized exchanges.

Decentralized Exchange (DEX)

A decentralized exchange allows users to trade tokens directly from their wallets without intermediaries.

Decentralization

Decentralization means that control is not held by one single company or authority. Instead, power is distributed among many users and participants.
