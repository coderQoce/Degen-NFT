Degen Handbook

A living guide to the $DEGEN community, token, app, and culture.

Topic 1

This Degen Handbook is a simple guide that will help people understand everything about the Degen (DEGEN) ecosystem.

It explains the community, the token, the tools, and the culture around DEGEN in a way that anyone can understand.

If someone is new to DEGEN, this handbook shows them how things work, how to participate, and how to become part of the community.

⬆️The Community

At the heart of DEGEN is its community.

The project grew from users on Farcaster, especially people active on Warpcast.

Instead of companies controlling everything, the community drives the ecosystem.

This handbook explains:

  • Who the Degens are
  • How people contribute to the ecosystem
  • How memes, discussions, and collaboration grow the project
  • Community rules and culture

In simple terms: The people are the project.

⬆️The Token ($DEGEN)

This handbook also explains the DEGEN token.

The token is the digital currency of the Degen ecosystem.

People use it to:

  • Tip creators
  • Reward good content
  • Participate in the ecosystem
  • Trade or hold

Because DEGEN lives on Base, transactions are fast and cheap, which makes it easy for social rewards and small payments.

The handbook explains:

  • What the token is
  • How it was distributed
  • How to store it safely
  • How people use it
⬆️The Degen App & Tools

The Degen ecosystem is not just a token it also includes tools and apps.

This handbook explains things like:

  • How the Degen App works
  • How tipping works on social platforms
  • Wallets that support DEGEN
  • Bots, dashboards, and explorers

These tools help people interact with the token and the community easily.

⬆️The Culture

DEGEN is also about internet culture.

The handbook explains the mindset of degens, which usually includes:

  • Humor and memes
  • Taking risks in crypto
  • Supporting builders and creators
  • Moving fast and experimenting

In the DEGEN ecosystem, fun and creativity are just as important as technology.

⬆️Why the Handbook Exists

Crypto projects can be confusing for newcomers.

The Degen Handbook exists to:

  • Help new users understand DEGEN
  • Show builders how to contribute
  • Explain how the ecosystem works
  • Keep knowledge organized in one place

Instead of searching many websites or posts, the handbook puts everything in one clear guide.
